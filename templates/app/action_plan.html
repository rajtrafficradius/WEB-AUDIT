03 / Method

How priority is calculated

Impact 30% · evidence confidence 20% · reach 15% · business criticality 15% · dependency urgency 10% · inverse effort 10%. Implementation risk is never hidden inside the score.

Because risk sits outside the score, a high-priority action can still be blocked until someone approves it at a gate.
